Stephen Cole

Early Bitcoiner & Serial Entrepreneur

Stephen is the CEO of Castle, a company helping businesses protect themselves from inflation with bitcoin. He's an angel investor in more than 80 companies and was one of the first bitcoin-focused startup investors in the world. After moving from Silicon Valley to Phoenix in 2020, Stephen founded the Arizona Bitcoin Network, which has grown into the largest bitcoin educational group in the state.

Notable Achievements

  • • CEO of Castle - helping businesses protect against inflation with bitcoin
  • • Angel investor in 80+ companies
  • • One of the world's first bitcoin-focused startup investors
  • • Founder of Arizona Bitcoin Network - the largest bitcoin educational group in Arizona

What to Expect from This Talk

  • • Insights from the early days of Bitcoin
  • • Lessons learned from building Bitcoin startups
  • • How businesses can protect themselves from inflation with bitcoin
  • • Career opportunities in the Bitcoin ecosystem
